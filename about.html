Concept Statement

729 Studio is a mobile recording studio that can provide live and studio recordings. We will come to you! We also provide studio spaces for recording or rehearsal. This flexibility allows you to get professional recordings in your comfort zone.

History

729 Studio was started in 2008 as a basement recording studio, then in 2016, transformed into a mobile studio concept. In 2017, we expanded to include a rehearsal space available for rent and the ability to provide live sound for events and shows.
